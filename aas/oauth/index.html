This page demonstrates how an OAuth flow can be incorporated into a web-based application.

Clicking on the link below will initiate the OAuth flow - a web-based application might do something similar as part of a configuration process.

When you click on the link below, you should:

  • Be directed to an Adobe Acrobat Sign Login page
  • Once you enter your credentials, you will be asked for your consent to permit the API Application to view your agreements
  • If/when you click on the Allow Access button, you will be redirected back to the e-sign.org.uk domain
